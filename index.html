Welcome!

Welcome to Alpine Rail Lodge, a cozy bed and breakfast nestled in the heart of the mountains. Surrounded by breathtaking views and inspired by the timeless charm of railway travel, or lodge offers a peaceful escape from the rush of everyday life.

You may be asking where we are located. Alpine Rail Lodge is located in the Sand Hills of Central Nebraska.

About Us!

Whether you are seeking adventure on nearby trails or a quiet retreat to relax and unwind, Alpine Rail Lodge is the perfect destination. Our thoughtfully designed rooms reflect the spirit of travel and exploration, combining rustic comfort with a sense of journey!

We offer great amentities, such as an amazing view of the mountains and the trains, which is perfect for photography!

Our rooms also allow for a beautiful sunset!

Come experience the beauty of nature, the nostalgia of the rails, and the comfort of a place that feels like home!
